Forged in Fire. Tempered in Battle.

Only the worthy may wield the blades of Gorvak Ironborn—legendary smith of the Molten Peaks. Each weapon carries a soul, a curse, or a blessing.

Legendary Weapons of the Ironborn

Soulrend

(Longsword)
This blade does not cut flesh—it severs spirit. With every strike, it drains resolve, memory, and will. Those struck may survive the wound, but often forget who they were before the blade passed through them.

Soulward

(Tower Shield)
More than protection, Soulward is a covenant of endurance. It absorbs not just force, but despair, fear, and curses—holding them at bay so the wielder may stand when all others break. It remembers every burden it has borne, and answers with quiet strength.

Soulpiercer

(Long Spear)
It halts fate itself. A true strike doesn’t wound—it anchors the soul, freezing foes between breath and death. Warriors impaled could neither flee nor fall, only watch their end unfold. Even battle’s echoes recoil from its shadow.

Soulbrand

(Mage’s Staff)
It channels raw ethereal power through a captured fragment of a dying star. Spells cast through Soulbrand do not merely burn—they unravel the threads that bind soul to body, leaving silence where magic once roared.

Soulstrike

(Recurve Bow)
An arrow loosed from Soulstrike always finds the truth hidden behind armor, distance, or deception. Its shots carry a shard of soulfire that lingers long after impact, haunting the target until they face what they’ve tried to flee—even if it’s themselves.

The Forge of Legends

Gorvak Ironborn was born in the ash-choked valleys beneath the Molten Peaks, where rivers run with liquid ore and the wind carries the song of hammers. At age nine, he quenched his first blade in dragon’s breath; by sixteen, he reforged the shattered Sword of Kings after the War of Shattered Crowns.

For fifty years, Gorvak has accepted only those who prove their worth—through trial, tale, or tear. He does not sell weapons. He bestows legacies. Every hammer strike is a vow. Every rune, a warning. And every weapon bears a soul: some benevolent, some vengeful, all unforgettable.

His forge lies beyond the Ashen Pass—guarded by stone golems and silence. Few find it. Fewer leave unchanged.
